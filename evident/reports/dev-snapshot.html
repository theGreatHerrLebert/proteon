Claim

Across N PDBs sampled (seed=42) from validation/pdbs/, proteon's CHARMM19+EEF1 single-point per- component energies at NoCutoff agree with BALL on the 5 gated components (bond_stretch / angle_bend / vdw / electrostatic / EEF1 solvation) at the same per-component bands the unit crambin oracle uses. proper_torsion and improper_torsion remain x-failed pending the topology / harmonic-improper compute followups. About 30-40% of randomly-sampled PDBs are skipped because the corpus contains nucleic-acid structures (CHARMM19 is protein- only) and a handful of free-CYS edge cases where BALL's atom typer can't resolve LJ pair parameters; these surface as a `skipped` record in the JSONL and are excluded from the headline statistics. A small fraction (<10%) of PDBs trigger a worker-pool segfault inside ball-py on certain malformed inputs; the runner uses a chunked ProcessPoolExecutor with a fresh pool per chunk so the crash doesn't lose the whole run, and the offending PDBs are recorded as `error` records.

Replay command: python validation/charmm19_eef1_ball_oracle.py
